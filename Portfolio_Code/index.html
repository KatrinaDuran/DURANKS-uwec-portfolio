About Me

Hi there! I’m Katrina, and I’ve loved photography for as long as I can remember. Even when I was little, I was the one always reaching for a camera — trying to capture the little moments that made me smile. There’s just something magical about freezing a feeling or a memory in a photo, and that’s what’s kept me hooked ever since.
Recently, I decided to take a big step and start my own photography business. It’s been such an exciting journey — learning, growing, and turning something I’ve always loved into something I can share with others. Every shoot reminds me why I started: the joy of capturing real emotions, genuine smiles, and memories that last. For me, photography isn’t just about taking pictures — it’s about telling stories and celebrating the beauty in everyday life. I’m so grateful I get to do what I love, and I can’t wait to help others see how beautiful their own stories are through my lens.
